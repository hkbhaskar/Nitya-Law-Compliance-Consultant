BIS Certification



BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) certification is the process that manifests the quality of the product of a brand, and manufacturer. It ensures that a product is safe and credible for the customer as it has cleared the BIS standards.

Our role in the process of obtaining the BIS certification would be the BIS certification consultant that will assist you from filling the application form, documentation, testing, coordination with the BIS officer or laboratory, so on and forth until you obtain the certification.

● BIS CRS

ISI Mark/ Certification Process


It is a product certification scheme commenced by the BIS. ISI mark ensures that your product has complied with the prescribed standards. ISI certification is mandatory to obtain for certain products without which you won’t be allowed to sell the product in India.

In a nutshell, it ensures high-quality products along with the safety of the consumers.

Below-mentioned is the process of obtaining an ISI Mark or ISI Certificate -

● Step 1
The application form needs to be filled.

● Step 2
Further, a factory inspection will be conducted for the assessment of manufacturing infrastructure, production process, quality control, and testing capabilities.

● Step 3
During an inspection, a product sample will be drawn for testing in a third-party laboratory.

● Step 4
To review, the test report of the product sample will be used.

● Step 5
Finally, you can obtain the ISI mark, if your product complies with the laid standards

To be noted, it is not easy as it seems, though, we effortlessly make this tedious process unchallenging because we possess the required experience and expertise in procuring an ISI certificate.
