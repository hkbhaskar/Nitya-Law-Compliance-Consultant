Section-8 Company Compliance


A section 8 companies is established in order to encourage and nourish the act of arts, commerce science, sports, education, research, social welfare, religion, charity, protection of environment or any such other objective. The Section 8 companies fall under the Companies Act 2013 which would be registered as an Non-Governmental Organization.

A company registered as Section 8 company cannot share profit made as a result of the charitable activities between its partners. The profit made during the functional part must be invested back into the company to promote trust and non profitable activities to give back. ection 8 Company has to follow the Compliances defined under the Companies Act, 2013.

It is essential for Section 8 Company to follow compliances prescribed by the Government. If a company does not stay updated with Section 8 Company compliances, they can end up paying the penalty to the Government.
